About Me

I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) with a strong interest in product development, entrepreneurship, and management. I’m deeply passionate about merging creativity with strategy—whether that’s through building scalable projects or crafting compelling design experiences. As a classical Kuchipudi dancer recognized by the Guinness Book of Records, Wonder Book of Records, and Telugu Book of Records, I bring discipline, dedication, and a unique cultural perspective into everything I do.

I co-founded LEAP (Leadership and Entrepreneurship Awareness Program), an initiative that bridges the gap between aspiring young minds and real-world business leaders. Through LEAP, I’ve interviewed numerous startup founders, entrepreneurs, and industry pioneers to extract actionable insights. These learnings are then transformed into research-backed content and shared with middle school students, nurturing the next generation of entrepreneurs with practical, experience-based knowledge beyond textbooks.

Additionally, I’ve worked closely with Tathastu Life, a psychological counseling center, where I led the branding and content creation—designing the logo, producing videos, and driving communication strategies. I also played an active role in operations and management, contributing directly to the center’s growth and outreach.

I believe in creating impact through action—whether it's initiating a program, designing meaningful content, or managing real-world operations. I'm always on the lookout for opportunities to collaborate, learn, and build things that matter.
